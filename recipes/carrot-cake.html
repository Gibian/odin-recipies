Isaac's Carrot Cake

This carrot cake is to die for!

After a delicious meal of chops some people enjoy a bit of a lighter desert. This carrot cake recipe
is perfect for an after dinner desert or a late afternoon snack due to its light and refreshing taste.
Personally best enjoyed on a hot day after a pool or beach visit.

Ingredients:

  • 3 c. all-purpose flour
  • 2 c. white sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 2 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • 2 c. shredded carrots
  • 1 (11 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained
  • 1 1/4 c. vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp. grated orange zest

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F (175° C). Grease a 9x13x2-inch baking dish.
  2. Whisk flour, sugar, baking soda, ground cinnamon, salt, and nutmeg together in a bowl.
    Beat carrots, mandarin oranges, oil, eggs, vanilla extract, and orange zest into flour mixture
    with an electric mixer until batter is smooth, about 2 minutes. Pour batter into prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean,
    40 to 50 minutes.
